How To Play

1

Swap adjacent gems by **clicking** (or tapping) two neighbors, or **dragging** a sweet in any matching direction.

2

Line up **3 or more candies of the same color** vertically or horizontally to blast them off the grid.

3

Matching **4 sweets** creates a Stripe Candy which blasts the entire column or row when matched!

4

Matching **5 sweets** yields an elite Lumina Nova gem which unleashes cross sparks to clear both horizontal and vertical lanes.

5

Complete the level's score targets before running out of moves! Use **Boosters** on the right of the screen if you get stuck!
